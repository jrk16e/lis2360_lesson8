How to Nail a Job Interview!

When applying for jobs always to your best to be extremely professional and prompt. After you had already applied somewhere to work at you usually will wait a few weeks to hear back about your application. Once hearing back you must prepare for your interview! Its important to be prepared before going to the interview so you're comfortable and able to be your best self. Sources say some of the most important things employers look for in a job interview are people who demonstrate eye contact, on time and prepared.

 

When it comes to being prepared there are a few things to do. Before going to the interivew study some information on the company so you're well informed of who you're working for. It is also important to identify something about you which you really want them to hire you. Not just something basic like a hardworker but something like a passionate employee who strives to work harder. Its important to relax and be positive about the outcome because there is no way to know what questions you'll be asked. One example of an off guard question is to explain your biggest weakness. However with practice before the interview you can stay calm and briefly think before answering. It is also important to dress in appropriate attire. For example never wear jeans and a tshirt to a job interview, dress up and look presentable.
